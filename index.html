🛣️ 2-Day Itinerary: Hosur → Chitradurga → Yellamma Temple

🚗 Total Distance & Travel Time

  • Hosur → Chitradurga: ~295 km | 5.5 hrs
  • Chitradurga → Saundatti: ~200 km | 4 hrs
  • Saundatti → Hosur: ~510 km | 9 hrs

📍 Day 1 – Saturday

🕖 Morning:

  • 4:10 AM – Start from Hosur
  • Breakfast stop around Tumkur (~7:00 AM)

🏰 09:30 AM – Reach Chitradurga Fort

Explore the fort, known for its massive boulders, ancient temples, and the story of Onake Obavva (2–3 hours).

🍽️ 12:30 PM – Lunch in Chitradurga

🚗 1:30 PM – Depart to Saundatti

Enjoy a scenic drive through rural Karnataka with optional short breaks.

🏨 5:30 PM – Reach Saundatti

Check into a lodge or dharmashala near Yellamma Temple. Rest or go for an optional evening darshan.

📍 Day 2 – Sunday

🕖 Early Morning:

  • 6:30 AM – Visit Yellamma Temple
  • Enjoy peaceful darshan and explore the surrounding viewpoints.

🍽️ 8:30 AM – Breakfast in Saundatti

🚗 11:30 AM – Start Return Journey to Hosur

Lunch stop around Hiriyur or Sira.

🏠 8:30–9:00 PM – Reach Hosur

🧳 Travel Tips

  • Carry light woolens – mornings can be chilly on the hills.
  • Book accommodation in Saundatti early – limited options available.
  • Fuel up before remote areas – fuel stations are sparse.
  • Prefer NH48 and SH routes for smoother travel.
